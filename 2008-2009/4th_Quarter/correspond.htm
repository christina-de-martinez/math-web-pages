Corresponding Angles


Another way to see if the triangles are similar is with proportions.

The angles that correspond are:
angle A and angle D,
angle B and angle E,
angle C and angle F.

So....
line AB and line DE correspond, also. Just like
line BC and line EF,
and line AC and line DF.

So if lines AB and DE are proportional, it is a similar triangle. For example, 2 and 4 are similar. 2 is a half of 4. 5 and 2.5 are similar. 2.5 is a half of 5. 6 and 3 are similar. 3 is a half of 6.

Therefore, the triangles are similar.

2 Properties


Commutative

To figure out a multipication problem, you can use the distributive property to make it more simple.
For Example:
(10x80)x3 is probably a lot easier to figure out using mental math than (80x3)x10.

Distributive


Another way you can figure out a multiplication problem easier is by using the Distributive property. You can rearrange how they are like this:
5(10+2)
it's easier to do 5x10, or 50, and then add it to 5x2, or 10, than to do the whole thing.
Then you end up with 60.
